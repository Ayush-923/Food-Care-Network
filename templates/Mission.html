The Genesis of Our Vision.

In September 2022, during our immersive learning experience at IIM Bangalore, a striking poster caught our attention, displaying the staggering daily amount of food wasted. This poignant encounter sparked a profound realization: while we couldn't individually halt food wastage, we could repurpose surplus food to aid those in need.

Motivated by this revelation, our team embarked on a journey to turn this concept into reality. Inspired by the poster's impactful message, we envisioned a solution that would bridge the gap between surplus food and hunger. This pivotal moment marked the genesis of our idea to create a platform where surplus food could find its way to those who needed it most.

From this humble beginning, our vision blossomed into a transformative initiative. Fueled by a desire to make a tangible difference, we channeled our energy into developing a website that would facilitate the seamless distribution of surplus food to communities in need. Today, our platform stands as a testament to the power of innovation and compassion, empowering individuals to enact positive change in the fight against food wastage.
